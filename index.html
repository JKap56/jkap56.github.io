Assignment 1 - Today's Mainstreet Prompt

The web is kind of like today’s Main Street—it’s where people go to find new businesses, shop around, and connect. Whether you’re selling something you made or offering a service, having a presence online is basically like having your own storefront that’s open all day, every day, for anyone around the world to stop by.

Business Idea - Diaper Service

People are become more environmentally conscious and backing businesses that help them acheive a more sustainable lifestyle with their wallets. Having too little ones right now I know I personally feel guilty with how many diapers end up in the trash. We tried reusable diapers, but the time to clean/reset everything for the following week on top of all the other demands of being the parent of a new born make them practically unfeasbale to use for any real stretch of time. A business that ships clean diapers weekly and provides a prepostaged way to ship back the dirty ones for laundering seems like an excellent business plan (if it doesn't already exist).
